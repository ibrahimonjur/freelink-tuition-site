The exercise

Three steps, no shortcuts skipped

Getting matched on FreeLink Tuition works the same simple way whether you're a guardian looking for help or a student ready to teach.

Q1.

Create your profile

Sign up as a guardian or a tutor. Add subjects, location, and what you're looking for.
